Webinars & Events


Mindspring provides free mental health education opportunities to the public through webinars on a wide variety of mental health topics. Webinars are offered for free each week with the generous support of Polk County, Polk County BHDS, and United Way of Central Iowa. All webinars are free and open to the public. Webinars are recorded and emailed to all registrants. Certificates of attendance are administered to anyone who participates in the live webinar and completes the survey at the end.
